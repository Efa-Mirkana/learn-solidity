# Presentation: Voting

On-chain governance and decentralized decision-making.

## Key Concepts
- **Sybil Resistance**: Ensuring one person doesn't have unfair influence.
- **Transparency**: Making every vote verifiable by the public.
- **Commit-Reveal**: A strategy for keeping votes secret until the poll is closed.

## Architectural Insights
1. **Delegated Voting**: The complexity of allowing users to pass their voting power to experts.
2. **Snapshot vs Live Voting**: Balancing the gas costs of on-chain voting with the security of live state.
3. **Incentive Alignment**: Designing voting systems that reward participation and punish bad actors.
